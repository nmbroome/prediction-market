## 2. What Prophet Is

**Prophet is a research platform.** Cassandra operates it to study how groups aggregate information, how forecasters calibrate, and how prediction markets succeed and fail.

Trading on Prophet produces the data that research runs on. Your participation is a contribution to that research, and you should understand that before you sign up. Section 6 explains exactly what happens to your data.

### 2.1 How the Market Works

Prophet uses an automated market maker. There is no order book, no bid-ask spread, and no counterparty to your trade other than the market itself. You buy and sell outcome shares against a liquidity pool, and the pool's composition sets the price. Prices are expressed as probabilities between 0 and 1. On resolution, shares in the winning outcome settle at 1 unit and all others settle at 0.

Nothing on the Platform is financial, investment, legal, or professional advice. Market prices are forecasts produced by participants and may be wrong.

### 2.2 The Platform Currency

Trading on Prophet is conducted entirely in an internal platform currency.

- **You cannot buy it.** The platform currency is not for sale. There is no purchase, deposit, buy-in, entry fee, or wager of your own money at any point. You cannot lose money on Prophet, because you never put any in.
- **You cannot cash it out.** The platform currency has no cash value. It cannot be redeemed, withdrawn, sold, traded, or exchanged for money, property, or anything else, whether on the Platform or anywhere else.
- **You cannot transfer it.** The platform currency cannot be gifted, sold, or transferred to another participant.
- **It is licensed, not owned.** All platform currency remains our property. You receive a limited, revocable license to use it within the Platform. We may credit, debit, adjust, expire, or discontinue balances at any time, including to correct errors or to remedy conduct prohibited by Section 7.

The platform currency is a research instrument. Its function is to make your forecasts costly, so that they reflect what you actually believe. It is not money, not a security, not a commodity, and not a store of value.

**Compensation is separate from the platform currency.** See Section 5.2.

### 5.2 Research Participant Compensation

Compensation may include a participation component and a performance-contingent bonus tied to forecasting accuracy. Compensation is paid in real currency, currently through PayPal, and previously through Amazon Mechanical Turk. **It is not a redemption of platform currency, which has no cash value and cannot be cashed out.** It is payment for research participation, calculated by reference to your activity on the Platform.

## 6. Research Participation

**This is the section that matters most. Do not skip it.**

### 6.1 The Public Research Archive

**When a market resolves, the individual forecast records for that market become part of Prophet's permanent public research archive.**

For each trade on the resolved market, the archive publishes: your Prophet username, the outcome you selected, the size of your trade, the market probability at the time of the trade, whether you were buying or selling, and the timestamp. Comments and reasoning you posted on that market are published alongside it.

**Your live positions stay private.** Nothing about your activity on an open market is disclosed until the market resolves. Your forecasts are yours alone until the answer is known.

The archive does not publish your email address, your payout information, your legal name, or any optional research information you have provided.

**The archive is permanent, and you grant us a perpetual, irrevocable, worldwide, royalty-free license to publish, reproduce, and distribute your forecast records and public comments as part of it.** This is what makes Prophet a research platform rather than a game. A forecasting track record that participants can selectively delete is not a track record, and a research archive that can be edited after the fact is not evidence.

Deleting your account does not remove your contributions to the archive of already-resolved markets. On request, we will disassociate your username from archived records where technically feasible, but the records themselves remain.

**If you are not willing to have your resolved forecasts published, do not trade on Prophet.**

### 6.2 Coded Data Sharing with External Researchers

Some research requires linking one participant's activity across many markets over time. That cannot be done from the public archive alone.

**Participation in Prophet includes this use of your data.** As part of the research you take part in by using Prophet, we may share a coded dataset of your trading history with academic and other researchers. Coded means your username, email, payout information, and name are removed and replaced with a research identifier. This is a condition of participating in Prophet, not a separate optional choice.

Coded data is not anonymous, and we will not tell you it is. We control it by contract: every receiving institution signs a Data Use Agreement prohibiting re-identification and redistribution, restricting use to an approved protocol, and requiring destruction at the end of the study.

You cannot selectively exclude your data while continuing to participate. If you do not want your data used this way, do not use Prophet. You may stop future use at any time by closing your account, but closing your account does not recall datasets already shared or research already published.

Full detail is in the Privacy Policy, Section 5.
